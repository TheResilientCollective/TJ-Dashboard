What We’re Doing

County of San Diego officials are working with partners and other agencies to assess concerns and impacts from sewage and pollution in the Tijuana River Valley and surrounding communities.

There are multi-agency efforts to address air and water quality issues in the Tijuana River Valley that are impacting San Diego area communities.
